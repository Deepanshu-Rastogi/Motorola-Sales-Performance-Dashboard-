# Motorola Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

I designed and developed this interactive sales dashboard using Power BI to analyze business sales, item quantities, and transaction trends from multiple angles. For this project, I handled the entire workflow—right from cleaning the raw data to designing the final visual layout.

## Key Features & Insights:
- **Core KPI Metrics:** Highlighted critical business metrics at the top, including Total Sales (769M), Total Quantity Sold (19K), Total Transactions (4K), and Average Order Value.
- **Data Visualizations:**
  - **Geographical Analysis:** An interactive map visual to track sales performance across different cities.
  - **Time-Series Trends:** Line and area charts demonstrating monthly and daily sales patterns to identify peak performance periods.
  - **Payment Mode Breakdown:** A pie chart showcasing customer preferences across UPI, Debit Cards, Cash, and Credit Cards.
  - **Customer Feedback:** A funnel chart representing the distribution of customer ratings from 1 to 5 stars.
- **Interactive Slicers:** Included monthly button filters on the left panel along with dropdown filters for Mobile Model, Payment Method, and Brand at the top for deep-dive data exploration.

## Tech Stack Used:
- **Power BI Desktop** (Data Modeling & Dashboard Design)
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- **DAX Formulas** (Custom Measures & Calculations)
